# 🏥 Healthcare Insurance Analysis
## 📊 Project Overview

This Tableau project provides an analytical view of insurance spending trends between 2017 and 2018.

The dashboard helps analyze:
- Insurance payment trends over time
- Commercial vs. Medicare spending
- Provider specialty-wise insurance payouts
- Relationship between patient copayment and insurance payments
- Quarterly state-wise insurance spending analysis


## ✨ Dashboard Features

## 1️⃣ Provider Specialty Analysis

Displays the **total amount paid by insurance** based on provider specialties such as:

* Internal Medicine
* Nurse Practitioner
* Orthopedics
* Dermatology
* Gastroenterology
* Behavioral Health

### 🔍 Insights

* Internal Medicine received the highest insurance payout.
* Behavioral Health showed the lowest insurance expenditure.



## 2️⃣ Commercial vs Medicare Comparison

Compares monthly insurance spending trends between:

* Commercial Insurance
* Medicare

### 🔍 Insights

* Medicare recorded the highest monthly insurance payment in **February 2018**.
* Commercial insurance payments remained relatively stable compared to Medicare spikes.



## 3️⃣ Copayment vs Insurance Payment Correlation

Scatter plot visualization showing the relationship between:

* Patient Copayment
* Total Paid by Insurance

### 🔍 Insights

* Weak negative correlation between copayment and insurance payment.
* Most insurance payments fall within the lower-to-mid payment range.



## 4️⃣ State-wise Quarterly Analysis

Bubble chart showing quarterly insurance spending across states:

* CT
* MA
* NH
* RI

### 🔍 Insights

* Massachusetts (MA) recorded the highest insurance spending in Q3 2017.
* Spending trends vary significantly by quarter and state.
